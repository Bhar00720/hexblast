How to Play

1

Match Colors

Click on a group of 3 or more connected hexagons of the same color to blast them.

2

Chain Reactions

When hexagons fall and create new matches, you score chain multipliers (x2, x3, x4...).

3

Build Combos

Clear groups quickly in succession to build a combo meter for bonus points.

4

Special Tiles

💣 Bomb = Clears all adjacent · 🌈 Rainbow = Matches any color · 🧊 Frozen = Requires 2 taps

5

Advance Levels

Reach the target score to level up. Difficulty increases with more colors and fewer moves.
